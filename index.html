Our Booking Policy:

All appointments made are considered official. Due to the volume of clients in the shop, your reserved slots may be given to walk-in clients if you are late for more than 5 minutes.

NOTES ON RESERVATIONS:

  • 1 appointment slot per person.
  • You may cancel or reschedule 4 HOURS before your reserved slot.
  • If you arrive late by 5 minutes from your appointed time, you may be waitlisted and your slot will be given to walk-in clients.

RESERVATION INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Select your barber. You may choose any barber with an available time slot of your preference if you have no preferred barbers.
  2. Select your service.
  3. Pick the time and date of your appointment.
  4. Enter your details.
  5. After you enter your details, take a screenshot of the notification and present this to the receptionist on the day of your appointment.
